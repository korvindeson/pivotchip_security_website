About PivotChip Security inc.

Dedicated to protecting businesses through ethical cybersecurity practices and innovative solutions

Our Story

PivotChip Security Inc. emerged from a need to supply custom devices that can support security testing engagement beyond the basic compliance level. Our founders recognized the growing need for trustworthy security professionals who could protect organizations without compromising integrity.

Our team members has helped hundreds of organizations strengthen their security posture, achieve compliance, and protect against evolving cyber threats.

Our Mission

To empower organizations with robust cybersecurity solutions while maintaining the highest ethical standards and fostering a culture of security awareness.

Our Vision

To be the trusted cybersecurity-testing device vendor, setting industry standards for responsible security practices and client protection.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Ethical Integrity

We conduct all security testing and consulting with the highest ethical standards. Client trust and legal compliance are never compromised.

Continuous Learning

The cybersecurity landscape evolves rapidly. We invest heavily in training and research to stay ahead of emerging threats.

Client Partnership

Confidentiality
