Custom Device Shell Design

3D-printed enclosures designed for discretion, blending seamlessly into any environment

Discreet Hardware for Real-World Operations

In red team operations and physical penetration testing, the ability to deploy devices without detection is critical. Standard enclosures can be conspicuous and raise suspicion. Our custom shell design service creates 3D-printed enclosures that transform your penetration testing devices into everyday objects that blend seamlessly into target environments.

We combine industrial design expertise with practical penetration testing experience to create shells that are not only visually appropriate but also maintain full device functionality, proper cooling, antenna placement, and access to ports and indicators.

Why Custom Shells Matter

A $3,000 penetration testing device is worthless if it's immediately discovered. Custom shells designed for specific environments dramatically increase operational success rates by:

  • Reducing visual suspicion from security personnel and employees
  • Matching environmental aesthetics (office, industrial, healthcare, etc.)
  • Appearing as authorized, expected equipment
  • Enabling longer-duration deployments without detection

Our Design Process

From concept to 3D-printed prototype in 2-3 weeks

1

Requirements Gathering

We work with you to understand the target environment, deployment scenario, and operational requirements. We'll discuss what the device should resemble, size constraints, mounting requirements, and any specific features needed.

Duration: 1-2 days

2

Concept Design

Our designers create initial concept sketches and 3D models. We'll provide multiple design options showing different approaches to the shell design, including dimensions, material recommendations, and aesthetic variations.

Duration: 3-5 days

3

Client Review & Revision

You review the concepts and provide feedback. We'll refine the design based on your input, adjusting aesthetics, dimensions, or functional elements. We typically go through 1-2 revision cycles.

Duration: 2-3 days

4

Technical Engineering

We finalize the technical aspects: internal component layout, mounting points, port cutouts, ventilation, antenna placement, and cable routing. We ensure the design maintains device functionality while achieving the desired appearance.

Duration: 2-3 days

5

3D Printing & Assembly

We 3D print the shell components using industrial-grade printers and professional materials. Parts are post-processed (sanding, painting, finishing) as needed. We assemble the device into the new shell and perform functional testing.

Duration: 3-5 days

6

Quality Assurance & Delivery

Final testing ensures all device functions work correctly in the new shell. We verify wireless performance, thermal characteristics, port accessibility, and overall durability. Device ships with documentation and assembly instructions if disassembly is needed for travel.

Duration: 1-2 days

Rush Service Available

Need a custom shell for an upcoming engagement? We offer expedited design and production with 7-10 day turnaround for an additional fee. Contact us to discuss your timeline.

Best For: Outdoor deployments, UV exposure

Properties: Weather resistant, UV stable, temperature resistant

Finish: Matte finish, excellent color retention
