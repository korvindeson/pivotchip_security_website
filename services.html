Why Security Education Matters

Studies show that over 90% of successful cyber attacks involve human error. Regular security training reduces risk by helping employees:

  • Recognize phishing emails and social engineering tactics
  • Follow secure password practices and use multi-factor authentication
  • Safely handle sensitive data and customer information
  • Report suspicious activity and potential security incidents
  • Understand their role in maintaining organizational security
